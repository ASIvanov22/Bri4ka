Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Bri4ka and how to use our car problem-solving platform.

How do I use Bri4ka to diagnose my car problem?

Simply type your car issue (e.g., "engine noise" or "oil leak") into our app. You'll get a visual guide and step-by-step solutions instantly.

Is Bri4ka free to use?

Yes, Bri4ka is free for all users. We aim to make car care accessible to everyone.

Can I get help from real experts?

Absolutely! Our platform connects you with car experts and a community of car enthusiasts for additional support.

What if I can't find my problem listed?

If your issue isn't listed, you can submit a new question or contact our support team for personalized help.

Is my data safe with Bri4ka?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously and use industry-standard security to protect your information.

Can I use Bri4ka on my mobile device?

Yes! Bri4ka is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and desktops, so you can get help with your car anywhere, anytime.

Do I need to create an account to use Bri4ka?

No account is required to access most features. However, creating an account lets you save your history, ask questions, and connect with the community.
